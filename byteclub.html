How to Play Byte Club

◆ Goal

Win by one of two conditions:

1. Hold the 🚩 Data Flag when ⏰ Times Up is drawn from the deck.

2. Play one of every attack type (Kill Chain) AND every defend type (NIST) in front of you, including the earned Govern and Action Objectives cards.

◆ Turn Flow

1. Play cards — click cards from your hand to play them. You can play multiple cards per turn.

2. End turn — click End Turn when done.

3. Draw — you automatically draw 1 card.

4. Discard — if you have more than 6 cards, click one to discard.

▸ Kill Chain — Attack Cards

ATK Recon — peek at a hand or swap played attack cards

ATK Weaponize — cancel an opponent's defend card (played out of turn)

ATK Delivery — swap up to 2 played cards with opponents

ATK Exploit — blindly steal 1 card from a target

ATK Install — block a target from playing defend cards

ATK C2 — steal the Data Flag, or target gives you a card

▸ NIST Framework — Defend Cards

DEF Identify — reveal who has the Data Flag or swap played defend cards

DEF Protect — block attacks until your next turn

DEF Detect — view and reorder the top 5 deck cards

DEF Respond — cancel an attack targeting you (played out of turn)

DEF Recover — draw until you have 5 cards in hand

▸ Special Earned Cards

GOV Govern — earned by playing all 5 NIST types. Look at all hands, take 1 card from each player, then give 1 back to each.

OBJ Action Obj. — earned by playing all 6 Kill Chain types. The Data Flag holder must give it to you. Cannot be cancelled.

◆ Tips

• Click the ⓘ icons throughout the game for quick help on any element.

• Dotted outlines in your played area show which cards you still need.

• Watch the game log for opponent actions.
